Dedicated to Excellence

Ijaye Highland Group of Schools is committed to offering all our students a broad and balanced curriculum enriched with rewarding and stimulating activities. This approach prepares them for a fulfilling social and cultural life.

Whether through our books or hands-on training, we ensure each student receives personal attention to excel in every subject, achieve better scores, and secure a brighter future.

Curriculum Overview

Our school's curriculum encompasses all the planned activities we arrange to foster learning, personal growth, and development. It includes the formal elements of the English National Curriculum as well as a variety of extracurricular activities designed to enhance the children's overall experience.

Computer Science

Our life goals are often shaped by our understanding of the world. Many children begin learning to code with the aspiration of creating games. We expand their horizons by demonstrating how studying computer science can open doors to careers in filmmaking and beyond.

Fine Art

The art and design curriculum is designed to ensure that all students achieve the following objectives: produce creative work that explores their ideas and documents their experiences, and become proficient in various techniques including drawing, painting, sculpture, and other forms of art, craft, and design.

Music

Music is taught to children from junior infants to sixth class. Students are introduced to music reading and writing, singing songs, and playing classroom instruments.

Mathematics

Mathematics can be understood as the science of magnitude, number, shape, space, and their relationships. It also serves as a universal language, expressed through symbols and diagrams. This discipline allows us to model and solve real-world problems, understand patterns, and make predictions.

Languages

A language-based curriculum offers well-planned content that is strategically presented to facilitate learning and enhance the language experiences of students across all subject areas. This approach ensures that students develop strong language skills while gaining knowledge in various disciplines, fostering comprehensive academic growth and effective communication.
